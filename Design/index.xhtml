Continuum

(Not Primary)

    

Surge: Each round, a character gains a random amount of SP between 0 and his Surge. Surge is based on Persona and Expertise.

         
                
      

Blaster: Deals multi-target damage, but usually needs help maintaining attacks every round.

         
      

Drainer: Reduces an enemy's effectiveness without primarily doing damage.

         
      

Obstacle: Prevents enemy strategies from working as planned by changing the battlefield.

         
      

Piercer: Deals single-target damage, often with ways to get around defenses.

         
      

Support: Provides bonuses to allies, often in the form of regaining limited resources.

         
      

Tank: Forces enemies to attack him and his strong defenses instead of weaker allies.
